From Energy to Healthcare Innovation

As a Representative and Technical Service Provider for Nelson Integrated Oil & Gas company, our leadership team presented MedLabSolve at Nigeria's premier Medical Laboratory Science Symposium marking a strategic expansion from energy logistics into digital healthcare.

The presentation, delivered on behalf of Chairman Ishegbe Nelson, showcased the technical architecture and vision behind MedLabSolve, demonstrating how precision, data integrity, and operational excellence hallmarks of our energy operations are now driving innovation in medical diagnostics and laboratory management.

Symposium Highlights:

  • Technical demonstration of MedLabSolve's integrated diagnostic platform
  • Live showcase of digital transformation in laboratory medicine
  • Recognition from healthcare professionals and medical scientists
  • Validation of cross-industry innovation approach

"Building MedLabSolve required the same engineering rigor we apply to fuel logistics precision, reliability and real-time data integrity. Excellence transcends industries."

— Company Representative & Technical Lead

The Vision of Ishegbe Nelson

Founded by Ishegbe Nelson (Chairman/CEO), Nelson Integrated Oil & Gas represents a commitment to building Nigeria's industrial future across multiple high-impact sectors.

What began as energy logistics has evolved into a diversified investment area spanning fuel distribution, retail power solutions, and now healthcare technology. From bulk diesel trucks to medical diagnostic platforms, we bring the same operational rigor and commitment to excellence across every venture.
